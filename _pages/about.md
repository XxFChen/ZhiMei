I am an environmental microbiologist dedicated to understanding and combating antimicrobial resistance in environmental systems. My research integrates cutting-edge molecular techniques with ecological insights to address one of the most pressing global health challenges.

My work focuses on **antibiotic resistance genes (ARGs)** in soil-water-plant systems, employing advanced approaches including DNA stable isotope probing (DNA-SIP), metagenomics, and high-throughput qPCR to unravel the complex dynamics of resistome transmission and horizontal gene transfer.

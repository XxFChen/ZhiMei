## About Me

I'm a **Marie Sklodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellow (MSCA PF 2024)** specializing in Environmental Microbiology at the **Catalan Institute for Water Research (ICRA)** in Girona, Spain. 

My research focuses on understanding and mitigating antimicrobial resistance in environmental systems, with particular emphasis on soil and water ecosystems. I employ advanced molecular techniques including DNA-SIP, metagenomics, and high-throughput qPCR to link microbial function with horizontal gene transfer dynamics.
